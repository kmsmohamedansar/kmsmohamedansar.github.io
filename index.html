About

I work at the intersection of analytics, data engineering, and solutions delivery. My focus is building reliable data systems that help teams make better decisions.

At Datasembly, I work with large-scale retail and CPG datasets, building SQL workflows, data pipelines, and dashboards used by internal teams and external clients.

Most problems start as messy business questions and end as structured data solutions built with SQL, Python, and modern analytics tooling.
